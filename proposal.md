# Major Project Proposal

## Descrition
This is a RPG style, turn-based game on a grid. 

## Needs to Have
- Five tile types
- Three screens
- Two types of enemies
- Very basic enemy movement ai
- Turn based combat
- Hold WASD to move, so no spamming is required

## Nice to Have

- > Two types of enemies
- Inventory
- Dialogue
